The model is checked against the following rules. Minimum area - Checks for units that have area lower than the minimum area. Similarly Minimum Edge Length - Checks for walls that are shorter than the minimum edge length. Finally, Minimum Angle Tolerence - Checks for angles plus or minus the selected angle over 90 degrees.
